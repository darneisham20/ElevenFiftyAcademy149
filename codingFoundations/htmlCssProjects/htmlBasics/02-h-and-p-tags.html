P tags can be used to house the main content of a page.

The Box Model - Content: is the area where the contents of your element are displayed (i.e. the text for the above P and H tags) - Padding: elemnt is the white space between the content box and the border for the element. This is extra space you want contained inside the element's border but that will not directly change the element's content. - Border: The border box wraps the content and any padding. - Margin: The outermost layer, wrapping the content, padding, and border as whitespace between this box and other elements.
